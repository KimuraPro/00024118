Classic Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

⏱ 20 mins 📊 Easy

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 100ml extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Parmesan cheese

Method

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add sliced garlic and chilli flakes, cooking until garlic is golden (about 2 minutes).
  3. Add the drained pasta to the pan along with a splash of pasta water. Toss well to coat.
  4. Serve topped with fresh parsley and grated Parmesan.

Artisan Sourdough Bread

⏱ 24 hours (incl. rise) 📊 Advanced

Ingredients

  • 500g strong bread flour
  • 350ml water
  • 100g sourdough starter
  • 10g salt

Method

  1. Mix flour, water, and starter. Rest for 30 minutes (autolyse).
  2. Add salt, then stretch and fold the dough every 30 minutes for 2 hours.
  3. Shape and place in a banneton. Refrigerate overnight (12–16 hours).
  4. Bake in a preheated Dutch oven at 230°C for 45 minutes.

Thai Green Curry

⏱ 35 mins 📊 Medium

Ingredients

  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 3 tbsp green curry paste
  • 300g chicken or tofu
  • 150g bamboo shoots
  • Thai basil leaves
  • Fish sauce and palm sugar to taste

Method

  1. Heat a splash of coconut cream in a wok until it splits. Add curry paste and fry for 2 minutes.
  2. Add protein and cook until sealed. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  3. Add bamboo shoots, fish sauce, and palm sugar. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Stir in Thai basil leaves and serve with jasmine rice.
